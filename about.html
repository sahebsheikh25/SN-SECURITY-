About SN Security

Practical tooling, guides and resources for security researchers.

Our Mission

To provide up-to-date, practical open-source intelligence and security tooling resources for researchers, defenders, and investigators — with an emphasis on responsible, ethical use.

What we do

We curate tools, tutorials, and practical workflows for reconnaissance, OSINT, vulnerability research, and defensive analysis. Our goal is to make quality resources easy to find and responsibly usable.

Responsible Use

All tools and techniques published are intended for lawful security research, incident response, and defensive activities. Do not use this information for unauthorized access, harassment, or privacy violations.

Contributors & Credits

This site aggregates and links to many community projects and open-source tools. Full credit to the original tool authors and maintainers; check individual project pages for license and attribution.

Contact

For corrections, partnerships, or to suggest tools, please use the Contact page or open an issue in the project repository.
